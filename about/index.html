Fernanda Mendes - Class of 2010

My specialization is in Human-Computer Interaction.

He as the President of LCSC for Fall'08 to Spring '09.

I joined the club because I knew that my involvement would have an impact in the direction of the club's future.

As our Computer Science department strives to become more diverse, I enjoy being a part of a community whose main goal is to support and retain underrepresented students in CS.
