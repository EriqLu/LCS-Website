About Us

We are a registered student organzation part of Department of Computer Science for Hispanics/Latinos as being underrepresented group in the Computer Science field.

Our oranization's major role is to target and support Latino/as who are interested to learn Computer Science that can apply to science, technology, engineering, mathematics, etc.

During the year, members involved through projects, hackatons, events, and meetings to build their resumes and meetings new people.

Everyone is welcome and to learn about Computer Science and Engineering overall.

History of LCS

The Latinx Computer Science Club became an official Registered Student Organization (RSO) in the Spring of 2007.

The club promotes students who share an interest in Computer Science and provides a welcoming environment for them to get to know each other.

What do we do?

Provide an office for studying, relaxing, socialize, and developing skills

Create and develop projects

Participate in Hackathons, conferences, and seminars from UIUC and other universities as a group

Organize tech talks, recruiters, company info, alums visits, and social events

Collaborate with other CS and engineering organizations events (ie. SHPE, NSBE, WCS, WECE, ACM, IEEE, etc.)

Build networks and friendships

Receive guidance and preparation for graduate school or internships/full-time jobs

Provide office hours for academic to personal assistance

Reach out tech and engineering companies for sponsorship and network (ie. Microsoft, Google, Facebook, Capital One, Wolfram Alpha, Cisco, UIUC Research Park etc.)

Benefits of Being a Member

Private technical skills presentations (e.g. Facebook)

Network with other Latinos/Latinas.

Tech Talks that provide great Internship/Full-Time opportunities.

Have a great time during social events!

Network with various company representatives

Plan several fun activities

Improve teamwork and communication skills

Eat free food at our events

Access to our office (Must be Active to maintain the office access each semester)
