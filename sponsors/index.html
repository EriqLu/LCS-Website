Sponsors

We thank our sponsors who help support our club running and help give back the community of color.

Without you, our club would never exist and the lack of diversity in the technical industries still continues.

Even though the United States and the UIUC Department of Computer Science still have a lack of students of color accept into the program, we hope the club would bring a message and huge significance of bringing under-represented students who are interested in computer science and engineering to be part of the program.
